Data Rights Policy for Weather Forecast Services

We respect your data rights and are committed to transparency about how we handle your information when using our weather forecast services in Rwanda:

  • Data Collection: We collect only the necessary data to provide weather updates and related services.
  • Right to Access: You have the right to access your personal data that we store and understand how it is used.
  • Right to Rectification: If you find any inaccuracies in your personal data, you have the right to request corrections.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Data Security: We use industry-standard practices to ensure your data is protected and secure.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Your data is not shared with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

If you have any questions or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our data protection team through our support channels.
