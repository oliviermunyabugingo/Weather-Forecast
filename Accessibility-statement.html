Accessibility Statement for Weather Forecast Services

We are committed to ensuring that our weather forecast services in Rwanda are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. This accessibility statement outlines our efforts:

  • Web Standards: We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) to provide a user-friendly experience.
  • Accessible Design: Our website design incorporates features that enhance accessibility, such as text alternatives for images and proper contrast ratios.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Users can navigate our site using keyboard shortcuts to facilitate access without a mouse.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Our site is designed to be compatible with screen readers to assist visually impaired users.
  • Feedback Mechanism: We welcome feedback on our accessibility efforts and encourage users to report any accessibility issues.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our accessibility practices, please contact us through our support channels.
